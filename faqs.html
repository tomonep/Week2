Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cakes, ordering process, and services

Ordering & Pricing

We recommend placing orders at least 48-72 hours in advance for custom cakes. For wedding cakes or large events, please order 1-2 weeks ahead. Same-day orders may be available for our signature cakes, subject to availability.

Our cakes start at $35 for small 6-inch cakes and can range up to $200+ for elaborate custom designs. Wedding cakes typically start at $150. Pricing depends on size, complexity, and decorations. Contact us for a detailed quote.

Yes! We offer volume discounts for orders of 10+ items or events with multiple cakes. Corporate customers and repeat clients also enjoy special pricing. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

We accept cash, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), and digital payments through PayPal and Venmo. A 50% deposit is required for custom orders over $100.

Flavors & Customization

We offer over 20 flavors including chocolate, vanilla, strawberry, red velvet, lemon, carrot, and seasonal specialties. We can also create custom flavors upon request. Ask about our flavor sampling service!

Absolutely! We offer gluten-free, sugar-free, and vegan options. We also accommodate nut allergies and other dietary needs. Please inform us of any restrictions when placing your order so we can ensure safe preparation.

Very custom! Our skilled decorators can create almost any design you can imagine. From cartoon characters to company logos, intricate flowers to architectural replicas - if you can dream it, we can likely create it.

Yes! We love working with customer-provided designs, photos, or inspiration images. Our team will work with you to recreate or adapt the design to work perfectly as a cake.

Delivery & Pickup

Yes, we offer delivery within a 25-mile radius of our bakery. Delivery fees start at $10 for local areas and increase based on distance. We also offer white-glove setup service for wedding cakes and large events.

Pickup is available during our regular business hours: Monday-Saturday 8AM-7PM, Sunday 9AM-5PM. We can also arrange after-hours pickup for special events with advance notice.

We provide sturdy cake boxes and detailed transport instructions. Keep cakes in air-conditioned vehicles, drive carefully, and refrigerate upon arrival if not serving immediately. We're happy to demonstrate proper handling techniques.

Storage & Freshness

Our cakes are best enjoyed within 2-3 days when stored properly. Buttercream cakes should be refrigerated and brought to room temperature 30 minutes before serving. Fondant cakes can be stored at room temperature.

Yes! Our cakes freeze beautifully for up to 3 months. Wrap tightly in plastic wrap and store in an airtight container. Thaw in the refrigerator overnight and bring to room temperature before serving.

Store leftover cake covered in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. Buttercream cakes should always be refrigerated, while fondant cakes can be stored at room temperature for 1-2 days if the weather isn't too warm or humid.
